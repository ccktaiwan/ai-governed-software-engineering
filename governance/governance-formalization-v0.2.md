# Planning

## v0.2

Focus:
Governance Formalization

Objective:
Formalize governance structures for AI participation in software engineering,
ensuring bounded authority, auditable decisions, and explicit review gates.

Scope:
- Governance layer definition
- Decision ownership and authority boundaries
- AI participation constraints
- Drift detection and enforcement models

Excluded:
- Operational playbooks
- Implementation guidelines
- Tooling or automation
- Educational materials

Rationale:
Operational guidance will be introduced only after governance constraints
are explicit and reviewable.
